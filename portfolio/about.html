I'm a freelance product designer and front-end developer based in Los Angeles. I graduated from UC San Diego in June 2025 with a B.S. in Cognitive Science (Design & Interaction) and am now pursuing my M.S. at the USC Iovine and Young Academy in Integrated Design, Business & Technology.

My path started junior year of high school as a marketing intern at a non-profit. Working closely with industry product designers, I became immediately fascinated by how they blended technical and creative skill sets to turn ideas into real, usable products. I never looked back.

I bring together a technical background, a creative mind, and a genuine love for people to advocate for real users while solving problems inventively. Five years from now, I hope to be part of a team of engineers, designers, and PMs building the future of technology. In the meantime, I'm dedicated to shipping impactful work.
